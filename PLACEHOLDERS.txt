Files that contain placeholders you need to replace before deploying:

  docker-compose.yml
  nginx-geoip/nginx.conf
  adguard-doh.mobileconfig
  .env.example  (copy to .env and fill in real values)

Placeholders:

  <xx_domain_xx>        Your DDNS domain         e.g. example.ddns.net
  <xx_server_ip_xx>     Your server LAN IP        e.g. 192.168.1.100
  <xx_lan_subnet_xx>    Your LAN subnet           e.g. 192.168.1.0
  <xx_data_path_xx>     Data directory on host     e.g. /home/user/OWN_DNS
  <xx_maxmind_account_id_xx>   MaxMind account ID
  <xx_maxmind_license_key_xx>  MaxMind license key
